Food and water are essential for life, providing the energy and nutrients our bodies need to function. Food supplies carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals, which support growth, repair, and overall health. Water, on the other hand, keeps the body hydrated, helps regulate temperature, aids digestion, and transports nutrients and waste. Together, a balanced intake of food and sufficient water is crucial for maintaining energy, preventing illness, and supporting every bodily function.
