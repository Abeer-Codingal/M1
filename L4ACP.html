Pakistani cuisine

Pakistani cuisine is rich, flavorful, and diverse, reflecting the country’s culture and traditions. It combines aromatic spices, herbs, and fresh ingredients to create dishes full of taste and color. Popular foods include biryani, kebabs, nihari, and samosas, often enjoyed with naan or roti. Each region adds its own twist — from the spicy curries of Punjab to the savory rice dishes of Sindh and the hearty meals of the north. Pakistani cuisine is known for its warmth and hospitality, bringing families and friends together over delicious food.

Generally, seasonal fruits serve as the most common form of dessert consumed after dinner. Pakistani desserts are sweet foods and dishes that are served with tea, usually during the meal, or at the end of meals in Pakistani cuisine.
