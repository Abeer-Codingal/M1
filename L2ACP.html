Horse Riding

Horse riding is an exciting and graceful activity that builds a strong bond between the rider and the horse. It requires balance, control, and trust, as both work together to move smoothly and confidently. Riding a horse not only improves physical strength and posture but also brings a sense of freedom and relaxation. Whether done for sport, travel, or fun, horse riding teaches patience, discipline, and respect for animals.

List of equipment requirements

  1. Helmet
  2. Saddle
  3. Bridle
  4. Reins
  5. Stirrups
  6. Girth
